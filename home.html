Who am I?

After touring around the world, from the US to Japan, Bobby Sant disappeared almost completely off the radar. He did some rare appearances, locked himself up and let new songs come back to him. Bobby expresses his music with sensitive sincerity. That's why his music hits you both in the head and the heart. His lyrics about being broken, hopeful and totally hopeless stay in the brightest spotlight. Bobby Sant's songwriting is constantly searching for those timeless melodies. It’s been his trademark ever since the debut album It's Just a Lonely Feeling from 2012 (with cover art from Townes Van Zandt's daughter Katie). - T.J.
